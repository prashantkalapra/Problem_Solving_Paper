  Match     Raid Points

Total Matches = 10

(a) Mean, Median and Mode

Mean

  Calculation                     Value
  ------------------- -----------------
  Sum of all points                 102
  Number of matches                  10
  Mean                  102 ÷ 10 = 10.2

Median

Arrange the data in ascending order:

6, 8, 8, 8, 9, 10, 12, 12, 14, 15

Since there are 10 observations,

Median = (9 + 10) / 2 = 9.5

Mode

    Score   Frequency
  ------- -----------
        6           1
        8           3
        9           1
       10           1
       12           2
       14           1
       15           1

Mode = 8

Answer: Mean = 10.2 Median = 9.5 Mode = 8

The mean is the most suitable measure for predicting the next match
because it takes every score into account and gives a balanced picture
of Sachin’s overall performance.

(b) Variance and Standard Deviation

    Score (x)   x − 10.2   (x − 10.2)^2
  ----------- ---------- --------------
            8       -2.2           4.84
           12        1.8           3.24
            6       -4.2          17.64
           15        4.8          23.04
           10       -0.2           0.04
            8       -2.2           4.84
           14        3.8          14.44
            9       -1.2           1.44
            8       -2.2           4.84
           12        1.8           3.24
        Total                     77.60

Variance = 77.60 / 10 = 7.76

Standard Deviation = √7.76 ≈ 2.79

A relatively low standard deviation shows that Sachin’s scores do not
vary too much from match to match, which suggests that his performance
is fairly consistent.

(c) Empirical Probability

Scores greater than 10 are: 12, 15, 14, 12

P(score > 10) = 4/10 = 0.4

The score of 8 appears 3 times.

P(score = 8) = 3/10 = 0.3

(d) Probability of scoring more than 10 points in at least 2 of 3
    matches

Let p = 0.4 and q = 0.6.

P(at least 2) = P(2) + P(3)

= 3 × (0.4)^2 × (0.6) + (0.4)^3 = 0.288 + 0.064 = 0.352

Therefore, the probability that Sachin scores more than 10 points in at
least 2 out of 3 independent matches is 0.352 or 35.2%.

Summary

(a) Mean = 10.2, Median = 9.5, Mode = 8.
(b) Variance = 7.76, Standard Deviation ≈ 2.79.
(c) P(score > 10) = 0.4 and P(score = 8) = 0.3.
(d) Probability of scoring more than 10 points in at least 2 of 3
    matches = 0.352 (35.2%).
